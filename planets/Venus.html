Venus

Planet

Venus, Earth’s ‘sister planet,’ is shrouded in a dense, toxic atmosphere and rotates retrograde once every 243 Earth days.

Distance from Sun: 108.2 million km (0.723 AU)

Moons: None

Surface area: 4.60 × 108 km²

Age: ≈ 4.5 billion years

Length of day: 243 d 0 h 0 m (retrograde)

Discovered by: Known since antiquity

Mass: 4.867 × 1024 kg
