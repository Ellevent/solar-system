Uranus

Planet

Uranus is an ice giant with a dramatic axial tilt of 98° causing it to ‘roll’ around the Sun.

Distance from Sun: 2.871 billion km (19.201 AU)

Moons: Titania, Oberon, Umbriel, Ariel, Miranda +

Surface area: 8.116 × 109 km²

Age: ≈ 4.5 billion years

Length of day: 0 d 17 h 14 m (retrograde)

Discovered by: William Herschel (1781)

Mass: 8.681 × 1025 kg
