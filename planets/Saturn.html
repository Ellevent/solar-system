Saturn

Planet

Saturn is a gas giant distinguished by its prominent ring system composed of ice and rock particles.

Distance from Sun: 1.434 billion km (9.58 AU)

Moons: Titan, Enceladus, Mimas, Iapetus +

Surface area: 4.27 × 1010 km²

Age: ≈ 4.5 billion years

Length of day: 0 d 10 h 33 m

Discovered by: Known since antiquity

Mass: 5.683 × 1026 kg
