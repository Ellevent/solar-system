Neptune

Planet

Neptune, the outermost planet, is an ice giant noted for its deep blue hue and supersonic winds.

Distance from Sun: 4.495 billion km (30.07 AU)

Moons: Triton, Proteus, Nereid, Larissa +

Surface area: 7.618 × 109 km²

Age: ≈ 4.5 billion years

Length of day: 0 d 16 h 6 m

Discovered by: Urbain Le Verrier & Johann G. Galle (1846)

Mass: 1.024 × 1026 kg
