Mercury

Planet

Mercury is the smallest and innermost planet of the Solar System, notable for its swift 88‑day orbit around the Sun.

Distance from Sun: 57.9 million km (0.387 AU)

Moons: None

Surface area: 7.48 × 107 km²

Age: ≈ 4.5 billion years

Length of day: 58 d 15 h 30 m

Discovered by: Known since antiquity

Mass: 3.301 × 1023 kg
