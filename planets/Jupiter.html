Jupiter

Planet

Jupiter is the largest planet in our Solar System, a gas giant known for its Great Red Spot and strong magnetic field.

Distance from Sun: 778.5 million km (5.204 AU)

Moons: Io, Europa, Ganymede, Callisto +

Surface area: 6.142 × 1010 km²

Age: ≈ 4.5 billion years

Length of day: 0 d 9 h 55 m

Discovered by: Known since antiquity

Mass: 1.898 × 1027 kg
